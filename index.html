About Us

Expedited Large Volumes Services Ltd

E.L.V Services Ltd. Is a fully registered company providing high-standard Civil Engineering and General Building Construction Services in Kenya. Since our inception in March 2018, we have delivered reliable solutions to our clients' most complex construction challenges. Renowned for our expertise in traditional construction methods and innovative approaches to modern technologies and delivery systems, we cater to a diverse range of projects. Our portfolio includes healthcare and industrial facilities, commercial, institutional, and educational buildings, as well as infrastructure projects like roads, water and wastewater treatment plants, and drainage systems. Our team of dedicated construction professionals ensures top-quality construction services, whether it’s restoration, site preparation, new facility construction, or facility renovation, all delivered on schedule and within budget.
